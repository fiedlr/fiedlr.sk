About

I studied Theoretical Computer Science at Masaryk University. Currently, I'm a Research Engineer at Runtime Verification Inc. We're developing matching logic, which is the theoretical foundation of the K framework. This framework allows you to generate all the relevant tools for your programming language automatically and reliably.

My software endeavors can be found on GitHub. When it comes to research, I'm primarily interested in mathematical logic and formal verification. This website aims to share some of my readable ideas with the world. I do not expect many people to read my posts, so take it as my public notebook.

If you are intrigued by what I do or have any questions, I would be more than glad to hear from you! Just drop me an e-mail or tweet me @fiedlr. You can also find more information in my CV.
